Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new class?

To create a new class, go to the Classes section and click the "Add Class" button. Fill in the required information such as class name, level, and schedule.

How do I track student progress?

Visit the Progress section to view detailed analytics about your students' performance. You can track individual progress, class averages, and specific skill improvements.

How do I create assignments?

Navigate to the Assignments section and click "Create Assignment." You can choose from various assignment types, set due dates, and assign to specific classes or students.
