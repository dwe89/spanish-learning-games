Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I start learning?

Choose your language (Spanish or French) and start with the basics section. We recommend taking a placement test first to determine your level.

Is this platform free?

Yes! All our basic content is free. Premium features may be available in the future.

Learning Process

How long does it take to learn a language?

Learning time varies by individual. With regular practice, you can achieve basic conversation skills in 3-6 months.

How often should I practice?

We recommend at least 15-30 minutes daily for optimal learning.

Technical Questions

Which browsers are supported?

We support all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Can I use this on mobile?

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices.

Account & Progress

How do I track my progress?

Create an account to automatically track your progress across all activities and lessons.

Can I reset my progress?

Yes, you can reset your progress in your account settings.
